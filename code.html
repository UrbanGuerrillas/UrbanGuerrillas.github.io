Principles

  • Identity First: The UG tag is worn with pride. If you wear it, you represent the clan everywhere.
  • Tactical Play: Coordination over chaos. Callouts, roles, and teamwork matter more than raw stats.
  • Respect: No drama in public. No trashing teammates. Handle problems directly and quietly.
  • Loyalty: UG isn’t a revolving door. If you’re in, you’re part of something with history.
  • No Cheating: No exploits, no hacks, no shortcuts. UG wins on skill and discipline.

How We Play

We favor small, organized squads. Everyone has a role, everyone communicates, and everyone understands the objective.
