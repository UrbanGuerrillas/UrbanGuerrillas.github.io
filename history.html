Urban Guerrillas — Official Clan History

Founders (The Beginning)

UG Jo Moma — The First Shadow, King of the M203

In the earliest days of online warfare, UG Jo Moma carved his legend in the streets of Delta Force: Black Hawk Down. His mastery of the M203 grenade launcher made cover meaningless and fear unavoidable. Jo Moma didn’t just play the game — he defined it, establishing the ruthless precision and tactical dominance that would become the foundation of the Urban Guerrillas.

UG Worm Pile — The M14 Warlord of the Rice Paddies

Before becoming UG MajBoyington, he was UG Worm Pile — the undisputed #1 Men of Valor player in the world. His arena was the rice paddies, where he called out entire squads with a simple challenge: “Meet me in the rice paddies.” Armed with his deadly M14, he ruled those fields like a warlord, snapping targets instantly and dismantling teams with unmatched precision.

Early Tactical Era (2003–2006)

The first wave of Guerrillas joined the founders, forming the earliest UG squad.

UG CoRrad — The Quiet Blade

Silent, precise, and deadly — the first recruit and an instant pillar of the clan.

UG Reapr — The Ghost in the Headset

A rumor before a voice, a shadow before a shot.

UG Tegu — The Wild Card Turned Warrior

Raw aggression refined into disciplined frontline power.

UG MILITANTGURU — The Tactician

The mind that elevated UG from killers to a coordinated, strategic unit.

Operation Flashpoint: Elite Era (2005–2006)

After the founders and early squad were forged, the clan entered its first true tactical crucible: Operation Flashpoint: Elite on the original Xbox. This was the first battlefield that demanded real military thinking — and UG didn’t just adapt, they dominated.

The Most Dangerous and Brutal Clan on Flashpoint

UG became the most feared clan the Flashpoint community had ever seen. Their coordination, precision, and overwhelming force turned every match into a one‑sided operation.

The Cobra Nightmare — UG Jo Moma & UG Worm Pile

Flashpoint players knew the warning: if you saw UG Jo Moma, it was already too late — because he was most likely in the Cobra gunship with his favorite gunner, UG Worm Pile. Jo Moma flew like a predator while Worm Pile shredded everything below with perfect tracking and ruthless efficiency. Entire squads vanished in seconds. Vehicles never stood a chance. Bases were erased with terrifying speed.

The community had a saying: “If you see the Cobra, you’re already dead.” But the UG version was worse: “If you see UG Jo Moma… Worm Pile already has you in his sights.”

Modern Warfare Era (2007–2012)

UG PUFF — The Young Blood Who Turned Warriors Into Mist

Entering the battlefield as the youngest Guerrilla, UG PUFF became a monster in Gears of War. With his signature shotgun, he moved with supernatural rhythm — sliding, flanking, and obliterating enemies so violently that they turned into mist. His brutality carried into CoD4, World at War, MW2, Battlefield, and eventually into the stars as CMDR UG PUFF.

Advanced Tactical Era (2013–2018)

UG Moko — The Unbreakable

Steady, disciplined, and unshakable — a rock during long operations and a force of consistency across every battlefield.

Frontier Era (2019–Present)

UGzorched — The Last Flame

The newest warrior carrying the torch into Elite Dangerous and beyond, bringing fire, aggression, and relentless energy to the modern UG legacy.
