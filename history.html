Urban Guerrillas

“First In, Last Out”

The motto that defined every operation, every deployment, and every member who carried the UG tag. It represents courage, loyalty, and the willingness to lead from the front.

Origins (2002)

The Urban Guerrillas were founded in 2002 during the early tactical shooter era. Built on teamwork, precision, and a no‑nonsense approach to squad operations, UG quickly became known for discipline, unity, and a signature aggressive tactical style.

Founders – Identity Lineage

The UG identity has always been preserved across platforms and eras. The founders carried their names forward into modern games while honoring their original tags.

  • UG MajBoyington
    Originally: UG Worm Pile
    Elite Dangerous: CMDR UG Col Boyington
  • UG Jo Moma
    Elite Dangerous: CMDR UG MOMA

This lineage forms the backbone of UG history and preserves the continuity of the clan’s identity.

Evolution of the Clan

Over the years, UG expanded across multiple games and platforms while maintaining its tactical roots. From early PC shooters to modern space combat simulations, the Urban Guerrillas adapted, evolved, and continued to operate with the same discipline and unity that defined the founders.

UG Timeline (2002 – Present)

  • 2002 – Founding of the Urban Guerrillas
    UG is formed by UG Worm Pile (later UG MajBoyington) and UG Jo Moma. The clan establishes its tactical identity and the motto: “First In, Last Out.”
  • 2003–2006 – Early Tactical Era
    UG becomes known for disciplined squad tactics, coordinated assaults, and a reputation for leading from the front.
  • 2007–2012 – Expansion & Core Member Era
    The roster grows with long‑standing members including CoRad, Reapr, PUFF, Tegu, MILITANTGURU, Major Dank, zorched, and Moko.
  • 2013–2018 – Cross‑Platform Evolution
    UG adapts to new games and platforms while maintaining its identity, structure, and tactical roots.
  • 2019–2023 – Elite Dangerous Transition
    Founders carry their lineage into Elite Dangerous:
    • UG MajBoyington → CMDR UG Col Boyington
    • UG Jo Moma → CMDR UG MOMA
    • UGPUFF → CMDR UG PUFF
  • 2024–Present – Preservation & Legacy Era
    The UG website is built to preserve the clan’s history, roster, lineage, and identity for future members. The motto remains unchanged: First In, Last Out.

The Modern Era

Today, UG continues to thrive through its Elite Dangerous wing, website preservation efforts, and the ongoing commitment to honoring the clan’s legacy. The motto remains unchanged — First In, Last Out.
