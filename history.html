Our History

Since 2002, the Urban Guerrillas have stood as a legacy clan built on loyalty, precision, and brotherhood. What began as a small group of disciplined players evolved into a long‑standing force across multiple games, platforms, and eras.

Founding

The UG were founded in 2002 by a core group of players committed to tactical gameplay, teamwork, and a code of honor. From the earliest days, the clan established a reputation for discipline and reliability.

Evolution

Over the years, the Urban Guerrillas expanded into new games, adapted to new systems, and continued to refine their identity. Through every transition, the clan’s values remained unchanged — loyalty, precision, and brotherhood.

Timeline

  • 2002: Urban Guerrillas founded.
  • 2004–2008: Expansion into multiple competitive titles.
  • 2010–2015: Establishment of Elite and Squad divisions.
  • 2016–2020: Modernization of clan structure and branding.
  • 2021–Present: Continued legacy across platforms and communities.
