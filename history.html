Origins

Urban Guerrillas began as a small, tightly organized tactical unit in squad-based shooters. The focus was never on recruiting everyone—it was on building a core that played together like a well-drilled team.

Leadership Era

Under the co‑founders, UG developed a reputation for structure and reliability. Ranks, roles, and expectations were clear. The clan’s identity solidified around the “UG” tag and the way its members carried themselves in-game.

Transition Across Games

As games and platforms changed, the roster stayed small but loyal. UG moved with its core players, keeping the same mindset: disciplined squads, clear communication, and respect for the tag.

Into the Elite Galaxy

In Elite Dangerous, UG re-emerged as a squadron operating in the vastness of space. The same principles applied: small, coordinated groups, a shared identity, and a long memory for those who flew under the UG banner.
