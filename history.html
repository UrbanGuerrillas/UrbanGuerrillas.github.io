Our History

The Urban Guerrillas were founded in 2002 with a simple purpose: to build a disciplined, loyal, and battle‑ready gaming unit. Over the years, we’ve evolved through multiple eras, platforms, and operations — but our core values have never changed.

From early tactical shooters to modern open‑world and space‑combat operations, UG has remained a force built on teamwork, precision, and unbreakable brotherhood.
